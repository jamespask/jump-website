JUMP

About

Experience the highs and lows of competitive showjumping in our captivating behind-the-scenes series. It is a sport in which there is no discrimination, where men and women compete alongside each other at the same events, at the same heights and under the same rules. The emphasis is on skill and viewers watch as rider and horse conquer obstacles and face defeat. The series will offer an in-depth and unfiltered look at the preparation, training and dedication required to excel in this intensive, exhilarating equestrian sport camaraderie that define the showjumping circuit.

The series will follow Jamie Gornall as he embarks on his journey towards the biggest stage in showjumping, the 2024 Paris Olympics. Viewers will follow him on his quest to represent the UK and will witness his emotional journey as he competes in various qualifying events and strives to achieve peak performance. Jamie's story will serve as a source of inspiration and motivation.
